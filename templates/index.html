Detectable Conditions

Our model classifies across four categories

🟡
● Moderate

Cedar Apple Rust

Bright orange-yellow spots caused by Gymnosporangium juniperi-virginianae. Requires two host plants to complete lifecycle.

🔴
● High Severity

Apple Scab

Olive-brown velvety lesions caused by Venturia inaequalis. Most economically damaging apple disease worldwide.

🟢
● No Disease

Healthy Leaf

Uniform green coloration, intact margins, and smooth waxy surface — all hallmarks of a thriving apple tree.

🟣
● Severe

Black Rot

"Frogeye" leaf spots and mummified fruit caused by Botryosphaeria obtusa. Overwinters in cankers and dead wood.
